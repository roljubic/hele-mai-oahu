Where to Live

Oahu's Top Neighborhoods
for Every Lifestyle

Not all of Oahu is the same. The right neighborhood for a young family is completely different from the right fit for a retired couple or a remote-working executive. We help you find yours.

01

Kahala

Oahu's most prestigious residential enclave. Quiet, private, oceanfront. Ideal for executives and wealthy families seeking maximum privacy.

Luxury ¬∑ Quiet ¬∑ Prestige
02

Kailua

Windward Oahu's crown jewel. Beach town feel, top schools, strong community. Best for families and professionals wanting a laid-back lifestyle.

Family ¬∑ Beach ¬∑ Community
03

Diamond Head

Iconic hillside and oceanfront estates with stunning views. Walking distance to Kapiolani Park. Preferred by the discerning wealthy buyer.

Views ¬∑ Iconic ¬∑ Walking
04

Hawaii Kai

Master-planned community with marina access, top schools, and a suburban feel. Very popular with families relocating from the mainland.

Family ¬∑ Marina ¬∑ Suburban
05

Manoa

Lush, green valley neighborhood near UH. Cooler temperatures, excellent schools, strong sense of community. Popular with academics and families.

Schools ¬∑ Green ¬∑ Community
06

Ko Olina

Leeward resort community with calm lagoons and luxury amenities. Growing residential area ideal for those who want a resort lifestyle year-round.

Resort ¬∑ Lagoons ¬∑ New Builds
07

Nuuanu

Historic, quiet, and cooler. Old Hawaii character with proximity to downtown. Ideal for those who appreciate history and a slower pace.

Historic ¬∑ Cool ¬∑ Character
08

Downtown / Kakaako

Oahu's urban core, rapidly developing. New condos, walkable, vibrant food scene. Perfect for young professionals and remote workers.

Urban ¬∑ Walkable ¬∑ Modern
